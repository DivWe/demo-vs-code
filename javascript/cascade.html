PURPLE ARMY REGISTRATION FORM

The Bangtan Boys, is a South Korean boy band formed in 2010. The band consists of Jin, Suga, J-Hope, RM, Jimin, V, and Jungkook. They announced that their name would also stand for "Beyond the Scene" as part of their new brand identity.This extended the meaning of their name to encompass the idea of growth "from a boy to an adult who opens the doors that are facing forward"
